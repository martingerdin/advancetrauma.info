About the Trial

Purpose

This study evaluates the effectiveness of Advanced Trauma Life Support® (ATLS) training compared to standard care practices in improving outcomes for adult trauma patients.

Study Design

Cluster randomised trial involving multiple healthcare facilities to assess the impact of standardized trauma care training protocols.

Outcomes

Measuring patient survival rates, treatment efficiency, and overall care quality in trauma cases across participating facilities.
